About Us

Our Genesis


We at "ABC" Learning Center strive to make quality education affordable to the masses. Over the years, the cost of education has increased many folds to make it beyond the reach of many. You may be appalled to learn that even in most developed nations students resort to prostitution to pay up for their college tuition fees. We want to remove this financial barrier to a good education. The only barrier to education must be an individual’s thirst for knowledge.



Our Founder



Lithan love technology and always had a flair for teaching. "ABC" Learning Center was born after his many iterations to make education easily accessible and fun. In his current role, it oversees the 360 operations of the company, leading a dedicated and committed team who share our vision.

About "ABC" Learning Center


"ABC" Learning Center is an accredited training organization endorsed by various government agencies under Singapore’s ministry of education. We are EduTrust certified by the Committee for Private Education (CPE) and a Centre for Continuous Education and Training (CET) appointed by SkillsFuture (SSG) Singapore.


We are recognized for our learning innovation with local and international awards including the Microsoft Global Learning Partner of the Year Award (Top 3) in 2018, Asia’s Pearson’s College of the Year (Bronze) Award in 2019, and AsiaPac CIO’s Top 10 Edutech Solution Provider Award in 2020, amongst others.


We have served over 50,000 learners and 2000 enterprises with an above 90% completion rate.
